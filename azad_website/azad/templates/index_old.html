Azad Hall of Residence

Being a member of the esteemed Azad Hall is a privilege that carries with it a sense of pride and belonging, evoking deep emotions in those fortunate enough to be a part of the Azad family. The experience of being an Azadian is one that is indescribable, leaving a lasting impact on those who are fortunate enough to call themselves a part of this esteemed community.

Let's try to know about Azad Hall !


  • Azad Hall is known for its fast-paced and energetic atmosphere, with a high tempo that inspires hard work and dedication from its members.
  • Azad Hall is a place where boarders can feel a sense of belonging and connection to their community. The Hall has a strong sense of identity and pride, and boarders who live there often feel a strong attachment to the Hall and its traditions.
  • Azad Hall is a place where boarders can find a variety of activities and events that cater to their interests and passions. Whether it's cultural events, sports activities, or community service projects, there is always something going on that students can get involved in.
  • The Hall is home to a range of resources and facilities that are designed to support student learning and well-being, including libraries, study areas, and recreational facilities.
  • The Hall is known for its welcoming and inclusive atmosphere, where students from different backgrounds and cultures can come together and form strong bonds of friendship and support.

Overall, Azad Hall of Residence is a place that generates positive emotions and feelings among its boarders, fostering a sense of belonging, community, and purpose.
