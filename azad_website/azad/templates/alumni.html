Why You Should Attend

Mentorship and Guidance

Alumni meets facilitate mentorship opportunities, where experienced graduates can offer guidance to those in the early stages of their careers. This mentorship fosters personal and professional growth for both mentors and mentees.

Lifelong Connections

Alumni meets foster a sense of belonging and a lifelong connection between graduates and their educational institution. This connection goes beyond academic years and contributes to a strong and supportive alumni network.

Philanthropy and Fundraising

Alumni meets provide opportunities for graduates to contribute to their alma mater through philanthropy and fundraising initiatives. A strong connection between alumni and the institution often leads to increased support for educational programs and facilities.

About

ALUMNI MEET

Alumni meet is a reunion where former students of Azad Hall of Residence, IIT Kharagpur Organized by Alumni Affairs, Azad Hall of Residence, IIT Kharagpur gather to reconnect and celebrate shared memories. The event offer a platform for networking, professional growth, and updates on the alma mater's progress. Typically featuring guest speakers, class reunions, and cultural activities, alumni meets foster a sense of community and pride. They provide a valuable opportunity for graduates to exchange experiences, receive career insights, and maintain a lasting connection with their educational roots. Overall, these gatherings strengthen the bond between alumni and their alma mater, creating a supportive and enduring network.
